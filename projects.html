Sales Performance Dashboard

Business Problem

Management needed a clear view of sales performance to understand revenue fluctuations and identify underperforming products and regions.

Dataset Description

Sales transaction data including date, product, category, region, revenue, quantity sold, and discounts.

Tools Used

Power BI, SQL, Excel

Solution Approach

  • Cleaned and transformed raw sales data
  • Built a star-schema data model
  • Defined core KPIs (Revenue, Growth Rate, Average Order Value)
  • Designed an interactive Power BI dashboard with filters by time and region

Key Insights & Results

  • Identified that 20% of products generated over 60% of total revenue
  • Detected regions with declining performance due to high discount rates
  • Enabled data-driven pricing and inventory decisions

Customer Churn Analysis

Business Problem

The company experienced increasing customer churn without a clear understanding of the root causes.

Dataset Description

Customer data including demographics, subscription duration, service usage, complaints, and churn status.

Tools Used

Python, SQL, Power BI

Solution Approach

  • Performed Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to identify churn patterns
  • Analyzed customer behavior prior to churn
  • Segmented customers based on churn risk
  • Visualized results in a dashboard for stakeholders

Key Insights & Results

  • Low-usage customers showed significantly higher churn rates
  • Customer complaints were a major churn driver
  • Provided retention strategies to reduce churn risk

HR Analytics Dashboard

Business Problem

High employee turnover was impacting productivity and operational stability.

Dataset Description

Employee data including age, department, salary, performance rating, tenure, and attrition status.

Tools Used

Power BI, SQL, Excel

Solution Approach

  • Analyzed attrition rates by department and tenure
  • Correlated salary and performance metrics
  • Designed an interactive HR dashboard for management

Key Insights & Results

  • Highest attrition occurred within the first two years of employment
  • Clear relationship between compensation and retention
  • Recommended targeted retention and compensation strategies

Marketing Campaign Analysis

Business Problem

The marketing team needed to evaluate campaign effectiveness and optimize return on investment (ROI).

Dataset Description

Marketing campaign data including channel, cost, impressions, conversions, and revenue.

Tools Used

Python, Power BI, Excel

Solution Approach

  • Calculated conversion rates and ROI per channel
  • Compared campaign performance across platforms
  • Identified high-performing and underperforming campaigns

Key Insights & Results

  • Digital channels delivered the highest ROI at lower cost
  • Some low-budget campaigns outperformed high-spend campaigns
  • Improved marketing budget allocation decisions
