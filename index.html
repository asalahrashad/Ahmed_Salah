Sales Performance Dashboard

Business Problem

Management needed a clear view of sales performance to understand revenue fluctuations and identify underperforming products and regions.

Dataset Description

Sales transaction data including date, product, category, region, revenue, quantity sold, and discounts.

Tools Used

Power BI, SQL, Excel

Solution Approach

  • Cleaned and transformed raw sales data
  • Built a star-schema data model
  • Defined core KPIs (Revenue, Growth Rate, Average Order Value)
  • Designed an interactive Power BI dashboard with filters by time and region

Key Insights & Results

  • Identified that 20% of products generated over 60% of total revenue
  • Detected regions with declining performance due to high discount rates
  • Enabled data-driven pricing and inventory decisions

Customer Churn Analysis

Business Problem

The company experienced increasing customer churn without a clear understanding of the root causes.

Dataset Description

Customer data including demographics, subscription duration, service usage, complaints, and churn status.

Tools Used

Python, SQL, Power BI

Solution Approach

  • Performed Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to identify churn patterns
  • Analyzed customer behavior prior to churn
  • Segmented customers based on churn risk
  • Visualized results in a dashboard for stakeholders

Key Insights & Results

  • Low-usage customers showed significantly higher churn rates
  • Customer complaints were a major churn driver
  • Provided retention strategies to reduce churn risk
